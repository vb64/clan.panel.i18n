Defense of Stronghold

Does you clan want to reap the benefits of high-level Stronghold bonuses, but don't want to be a "feeder" for top clans? Or have you already had such negative experience?

Theoretically, there is a solution - join with other clans and cover each other's SH's by not letting enemies attack. However, everyone who has tried this knows about tons of issues with this strategy.

A clan panel's "Stronghold" will help solve most of these problems.

Each member of an alliance will get an individual list of tasks based on rules that are very hard to compose manually. This is a dynamic, flexible list that automatically updates according to the activities of other alliance members.

The data are always up-to-date - if their age exceeds 10 minutes, they are automatically recalculated using the WG clan portal.

Alliance members can always check which clans can cover them on a particular day.

There are also tips about the time remaining until new attacks on alliance members and your own clan.

The alliance summary will automatically reveal "fat cats" who are not doing what they must to provide the much-needed cover for alliance members.

A detailed description is available.

And yes, it's totally free.
